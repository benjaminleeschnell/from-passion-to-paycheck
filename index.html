About Jasmine

Jasmine M. Clark is on a mission to alter how college students, institutions and corporations view life after school.

A graduate of NYU Stern School of Business and former Unilever Future Leader brand marketer, she transitioned JMC Marketing from freelance services here and there in NYC to an independent strategic marketing services firm based in Chattanooga, TN in the summer of 2015.

Inspired by her own story of recognizing her value and communicating that effectively to create a life she excitedly wakes up to every day, she created and facilitated From Passion To Paycheck: Freelance 101 during Chattanooga’s Start-Up Week in October 2015. The workshop attracted a diverse group of entrepreneurs, professionals, and doers, while garnering high praise.

This spring, she kicks off the official launch of From Passion To Paycheck with a promotional tour at elite universities.
